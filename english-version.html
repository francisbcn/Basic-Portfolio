Welcome to my portfolio!

My name is Francis, I'm a fashion designer, industrial pattern maker, and Junior Front-End Web Developer living in Barcelona, Spain. I have a strong foundation in fashion design and garment construction, as well as experience in creating and maintaining websites.

I am also a self-taught graphic designer and social media manager, and have a passion for entrepreneurship. I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to showcase my skills.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio website. I hope you enjoy exploring my work.

About Me

I started my career in fashion in 2013 and launched my first inclusive clothing brand produced in Barcelona, called "The Milk Barcelona", in 2018. Since then, I have worked on numerous fashion projects. In 2020, I founded my second bespoke couture clothing brand called "PamaGarcia" and specialized in this field. I have acquired skills in 3D fashion design software, graphic design, and pattern-making, such as CLO3D, GERBER, Photoshop, and Illustrator, allowing me to offer a complete and quality service. In 2022, I decided to pursue a career in web development to blend code and fashion and create futuristic and avant-garde designs. Since then, I have been studying front-end and back-end development.

I enjoy creating a cohesive brand identity for my projects and bringing creative ideas to life. I am always looking for new ways to improve my skills and share my knowledge through my creative work. I am excited to see where my passions for fashion, design, and technology take me in the future. Thank you for reading and learning more about my journey. Tienda

The Milk Barcelona

The Milk Barcelona is an inclusive and locally-made clothing brand based in Barcelona. Since 2018, I have been designing garments that reflect my love for the city, its culture, and its people. Our products are available on platforms like Etsy and Amazon.

With a strong emphasis on quality, comfort, and durability, my clothing is crafted to meet the highest standards. I take pride in being inclusive and representing the vibrant Barcelona community through my designs.

As the sole creator and owner of The Milk Barcelona, I have been personally responsible for every aspect of the brand. From conceptualizing the name to developing the branding, designing the products, ensuring top-notch quality, managing production, creating captivating visual content, developing the website, making strategic decisions, and more – I have handled it all.
