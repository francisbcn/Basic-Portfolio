About Me

I am a multi-talented designer and developer with a passion for fashion and technology. I started my career in fashion in 2013 and launched my first fashion project, a clothing brand called "The Milk Barcelona," in 2018. Since then, I have worked on numerous fashion projects, gained specialization in haute couture, and gained proficiency in design software like CLO3D, GERBER, Photoshop, and Illustrator. In 2022, I decided to pursue a career in web development and have been studying front-end and back-end development since then.

I enjoy creating a cohesive brand identity for my projects and bringing creative ideas to life. I am always looking for new ways to improve my skills and share my knowledge through my creative work. I am excited to see where my passions for fashion, design, and technology take me in the future. Thank you for reading 😊 and learning more about my journey.
