Welcome to my portfolio!

I am a fashion designer, pattern maker, and full-stack web developer based in Barcelona, Spain. I have a strong foundation in fashion design and garment construction, as well as experience in creating and maintaining websites.

I am also a self-taught graphic designer and community manager. I am passionate about entrepreneurship and constantly seek new challenges and opportunities to showcase my skills.

About Me

I am a multifaceted designer and developer with a passion for fashion and technology. I began my career in fashion in 2013 and launched my first fashion project, a clothing brand called "The Milk Barcelona," in 2018.

I specialize in haute couture and have acquired skills in design software such as CLO3D, GERBER, Photoshop, and Illustrator. In 2022, I decided to pursue a career in web development and have been studying front-end and back-end development since then.

I enjoy creating a cohesive brand identity for my projects and bringing creative ideas to life. I am always looking for new ways to improve my skills and share my knowledge through my creative work. I am excited to see where my passions for fashion, design, and technology take me in the future. Thank you for reading and learning more about my journey.
